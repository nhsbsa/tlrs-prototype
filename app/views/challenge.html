Notes on how the prototype works

November 2017 - The prototype currently handles all scenarios except universal credit or war pensions. The references below show the most common journeys.

1. PCN Numbers:
Enter a PCN number (from the list below) or leave blank to view Michelle Doe's penalty info.

2. Exemption reference numbers:
When asked for an exemption certificate number enter one of the following:

1. Match - "found"
2. Info is different - "we need to contact you about the difference in information"
3. Out of date - "certificate out of date"
4. cant find - "we cant find your certificate"

We need proof you were receiving Income Support or income-related Employment and Support Allowance between 1 November and 30 November 2017

What to do next

Type of proof we need to see

We need a copy of one of the following:

  • an Income Support or income-related Employment and Support Allowance award letter
  • a letter from the Department of Work and Pensions
  • a letter from the Jobcentre

If you were not receiving Income Support or income-related Employment and Support Allowance

Although you didn’t have a valid maternity exemption certificate when you claimed a free prescription, you still have the chance to apply.

What to do next

You have the following options:

  • Pay the total amount of £68.60.
  • Pay the original £8.60 prescription charge, and apply for a maternity exemption certificate.

If you were not entitled to free prescriptions but have an exceptional reason why you made the claim, you can ask us to review your Penalty Charge Notice. Find out if you can ask for a review.

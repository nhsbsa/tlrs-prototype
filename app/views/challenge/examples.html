How to send your proof

It’s as easy as sending a photo on your phone

  1. 1

    Take a photo of your proof using a mobile phone or digital camera.

  2. 2

    Check the image is clear and you can read the information.

  3. 3

    Send the photo as an email attachment to: {% if (ticked == 'K') %} nhsbsa.decs@nhsbsa.nhs.uk {% else %} nhsbsa.pecs@nhsbsa.nhs.uk {% endif %}

    You must include your name and Penalty Charge Reference number {{ref}}.

    We need to be able to confirm your identity, so your email must also include your:

    • First name and surname (family name)
    • Date of birth
    • Address including postcode
  4. 4

    Once we receive your email with your proof, we will check it and contact you within 2 working days.

Or you can send your proof by post

If you don’t have access to a digital camera you can photocopy your proof and send the copy to us. You must also include the following information:

  • Your Penalty Charge Notice reference number {{ref}}
  • First name and surname (family name)
  • Date of birth
  • Address including postcode

Pay or Challenge a Penalty Charge Notice

What happens next?

We will guide you through some simple online questions. We will:

  • ask for some details about the exemption or benefit ticked on your prescription
  • ask about other exemptions or benefits you may have had, as this might be easier for us to check
  • tell you how to send us proof if we can’t confirm your exemption straight away
  • Let you know the next steps

If we can confirm that you held a valid exemption at the time, your Penalty Charge Notice will be cancelled. You will not have to pay any charges.

How to send proof

It’s as easy as sending a photo on your phone

If you have access to a digital camera or a mobile phone with a camera, you can take a digital photo of your proof. Make sure the image you have taken is clear and send it as an email attachment.

Send the image to our email address. You must include your Penalty Charge Notice reference number 01234567890 in the email.

What happens next

Once we receive your proof we will check it and then contact you with a response. If you email your proof, we will contact you within 2 working days. If you send it by post, it will be within 5 working days. No additional charges will be added during this time.

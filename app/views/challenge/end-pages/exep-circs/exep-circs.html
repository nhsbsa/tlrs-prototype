Challenge a Penalty Charge Notice

Who can ask for a Penalty Charge Notice review?

We understand that you may have made an honest mistake and didn’t mean to do anything wrong.

If you have an exceptional reason why you claimed a free prescription you weren't entitled to, you can ask for a review.

Reasons we can't accept

We cannot review your Penalty Charge Notice if you:

  • thought your NHS exemption certificate was valid, but it had expired
  • thought your benefit entitled you to claim
  • thought your prescription prepayment certificate would automatically renew
  • feel you were misadvised by staff at your GP surgery or pharmacy

What to do next

If you have an exceptional reason why you made the claim at the time, you can contact us to review your Penalty Charge Notice.

If you’re not entitled to a review

You must pay the total amount of your Penalty Charge Notice.

Pay or Challenge a Penalty Charge Notice

Use this service to:

  • pay your Penalty Charge Notice
  • make a challenge

This should take less than 5 minutes.

Are you on Universal Credits?

If you have recieved a Penalty Charge Notice letter and are getting Universal Credits, you can't challenge your penalty charge online yet. Instead you should call us on 0300 330 9291 to discuss your letter.


Before you start

You will need your reference number. This can be found in the top left-hand corner of your Penalty Charge Notice letter.

To confirm your identity we will ask for your date of birth and the total amount on your Penalty Charge Notice.

Have you lost your Penalty Charge Notice?

If you have lost your Penalty Charge Notice or don’t know your reference number, call us on 0300 330 9291

How we use your information

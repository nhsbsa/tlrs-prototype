Pay or Challenge a Penalty Charge Notice

Use this service to:

  • pay your Penalty Charge Notice
  • make a challenge

This should take less than 5 minutes.

Are you on Universal Credit or HC3 (limited help with health costs)? Important information

To challenge your Penalty Charge Notice you will need to phone or email us.

You need to include your reference number. This is in the top left-hand corner of your Penalty Charge Notice letter:

For prescriptions

  • Call us on 0300 330 9291 (this call will be charged at a local rate)
  • Email us at nhsbsa.pecs@nhsbsa.nhs.uk

For dental treatment

  • Call us on 0300 330 1293 (this call will be charged at a local rate)
  • Email us at nhsbsa.decs@nhsbsa.nhs.uk

Before you start

You will need your reference number. This can be found in the top left-hand corner of your Penalty Charge Notice letter.

To confirm your identity we will ask for your date of birth and the total amount on your Penalty Charge Notice.

Have you lost your Penalty Charge Notice?

If you have lost your Penalty Charge Notice or don’t know your reference number, call us on 0300 330 9291

How we use your information

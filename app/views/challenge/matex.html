Challenge a Penalty Charge Notice

We cannot find your maternity exemption certificate

We cannot find your maternity exemption certificate for the dates between 1 November and 30 November 2017.

But you can still apply if you were pregnant when you claimed the free prescription (the date on your Penalty Charge Notice), or you had given birth in the previous 12 months.

What to do next

You have the following options:

  • Pay the total amount of £68.60.
  • Pay the original £8.60 prescription charge, and apply for a maternity exemption certificate.
  • Send us proof to show that you did have a valid maternity exemption certificate between 1 November and 30 November 2017.

If you were not entitled to free prescriptions but have an exceptional reason why you made the claim, you can ask us to review your Penalty Charge Notice. Find out if you can ask for a review.
